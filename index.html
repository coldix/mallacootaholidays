Mallacoota Holidays

Welcome to your local guide for an unforgettable holiday in Mallacoota—Victoria’s breathtaking seaside retreat, where ancient forests meet pristine coastline. Discover the warmth of a vibrant community and the beauty of nature at its finest.

Why Visit Mallacoota?

  • Enjoy secluded beaches, crystal-clear lakes, and lush bushland.
  • Fish, bushwalk Croajingolong National Park, spot wildlife, or simply soak in a lakeside view.
  • Whether you seek adventure, creativity, or pure relaxation, Mallacoota offers it all.

Stay in Mallacoota

Choose from family holiday homes, waterfront cabins, motels, and caravan parks for every budget and style.

Savor the flavors of Mallacoota—fresh oysters, lakeside cafes, cozy pubs, and local dining favorites.

Things to Do
